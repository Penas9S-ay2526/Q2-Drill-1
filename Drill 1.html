EMERGENCY GO BAG ESSENTIALS

Food & Water

Have at least one liter of drinking water and many non-perishable foods such as canned goods, crackers, and energy bars

First Aid

Bring a first aid kit that has basic medical supplies such as pain relievers, bandages/bandaids, and gauze.

Flashlight

Have a flashlight to see clearly in dark places and send distress signals in case resuers are around. It is also recommended to bring batteries.

Extra Clothing

Bring clothes for both warm and cold temperatures.
